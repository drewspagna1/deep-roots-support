## Frequently Asked Questions

**How are my numbers calculated?**
Deep Roots uses your full birth name and birth date to calculate your core numerology numbers using the Pythagorean method, the most widely used system in Western numerology.

**Is my data private?**
Yes. Your name, birth date, and journal entries are stored securely using Supabase and are never shared or sold. See our [Privacy Policy](https://www.termsfeed.com/live/2ac0fb7b-f577-4cba-aa06-41d1700450b3) for full details.

**What is included in the free version?**
The free version includes access to your Life Path number, limited daily readings, and limited journal entries. Premium unlocks full readings, extended chart details, unlimited journal history, crystal guidance, compatibility, and more.

**How do I subscribe to Deep Roots Premium?**
Open the app and tap the upgrade prompt on any gated feature, or navigate to your Profile and tap Upgrade. Subscriptions are available monthly or annually and are processed securely through Apple's App Store.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Go to your iPhone's **Settings -> [Your Name] -> Subscriptions**, find Deep Roots, and tap Cancel Subscription. Your premium access remains active until the end of the current billing period.

**How do I restore my purchases after reinstalling?**
Open the app and navigate to the paywall screen, then tap **Restore Purchases** at the bottom.

**How do I delete my account?**
Email us at drew.spagna@gmail.com with your request and we will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 7 days.

**The app isn't working. What should I do?**
Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, delete and reinstall from the App Store. If it still isn't working, email us with a description of the problem and your device model and iOS version.

**My daily forecast isn't updating. What's wrong?**
Daily forecasts reset at midnight based on your local time zone. Make sure your device's date and time are set automatically. If the issue persists, force-close and reopen the app.

**Can I use Deep Roots on multiple devices?**
Yes. Sign in with the same account on any device and your profile and journal will sync automatically.
