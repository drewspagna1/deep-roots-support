## Frequently Asked Questions

**How are my numbers calculated?**
Deep Roots uses your full birth name and birth date to calculate your Life Path, Destiny, and Personal Day numbers based on traditional Pythagorean numerology.

**Is my data private?**
Yes. Your name, birth date, and journal entries are stored securely and never shared or sold.

**How do I delete my account?**
Email us at drew.spagna@gmail.com and we will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 7 days.

**The app isn't working. What should I do?**
Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, email us with a description of the problem and what device you're using.
